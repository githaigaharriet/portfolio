# 📅 Calendar & Scheduling Management Sample

## 📌 Project Overview
This project demonstrates how I efficiently organize appointments, meetings, and deadlines for busy professionals. The goal is to ensure no conflicts, prioritize important events, and improve overall time management.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Keep calendars organized and easy to read  
- Avoid scheduling conflicts  
- Ensure reminders and follow-ups are set for important tasks  

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Google Calendar  
- Microsoft Outlook  
- Zoom / Google Meet (for online meetings)  
- Reminder and task management apps (optional)
- Notion

---

## ⚙️ Process
1. Collected all recurring meetings and one-time appointments  
2. Created a **color-coded system** for different types of events:
   - Urgent / High Priority  
   - Regular Meetings  
   - Personal / Miscellaneous  
3. Added meeting details, agenda, and relevant links  
4. Set **automated reminders** 15–30 minutes before each event  
5. Shared calendar access with team members for collaboration  
6. Checked daily and weekly schedules for conflicts or overlaps  

---

## ✅ Outcome
- Clear and organized calendar  
- No missed appointments  
- Improved productivity and time management  
- Easy collaboration with team members
