# 🎨 Social Media & Canva Project Sample

## 📌 Project Overview
This project demonstrates how I create visually appealing social media content using Canva. It highlights skills in design, planning, and content organization.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Design engaging social media posts for a brand or client  
- Maintain brand consistency and aesthetics  
- Plan and schedule posts for optimal engagement  

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Canva  
- Instagram / Facebook (simulated posting)  
- Google Calendar or scheduling tool for planning  
- Notion

---

## ⚙️ Process
1. Identified target audience and content goals  
2. Chose post format (Instagram post, story, banner, etc.)  
3. Selected visuals, colors, and fonts consistent with brand  
4. Added text and call-to-action clearly and attractively  
5. Scheduled posts in a calendar for consistent posting  
6. Reviewed and refined designs for clarity and impact  

---

## ✅ Outcome
- Clear and visually appealing social media content  
- Consistent branding and messaging  
- Organized posting schedule  
- Demonstrates creativity and design skills
